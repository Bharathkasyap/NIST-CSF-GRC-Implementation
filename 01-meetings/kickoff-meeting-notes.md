## Key Discussion Points

### 1. Business Case & Drivers

CISO Sarah Chen opened by outlining three primary drivers:

- **Regulatory Pressure:** FFIEC cybersecurity examination scheduled for Q3. External firm rated TechNova at 1.5/4.0 across NIST CSF — below industry average of 2.2 for financial institutions of similar size.
- **Threat Landscape:** Two recent incidents at peer institutions involving ransomware caused significant disruption. TechNova's detection capabilities are insufficient.
- **Growth Objectives:** Planned M&A activity in the next 18 months requires a demonstrably mature security program for due diligence.

**Board Mandate:** The Board Security Committee approved $2.4M for this 12-month initiative.

### 2. Project Scope — Agreed

- **In Scope:** All 275 IT systems in CMDB; all processes rated High or Critical in BIA; all 47 third-party vendors with data access
- **Out of Scope:** TechNova Mexico subsidiary (separate project, Year 2)
- **Framework:** NIST CSF 2.0 + NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5

### 3. Objectives — Approved

| Objective | Success Metric | Target |
|-----------|---------------|--------|
| Improve NIST CSF maturity | Average maturity score | 1.5 to 3.0 |
| Reduce critical vulnerabilities | Open critical/high CVEs | 106 to under 20 |
| Achieve FFIEC CAT target | FFIEC CAT level | Baseline to Intermediate |
| Reduce phishing susceptibility | Click rate | 32% to under 10% |
| Develop IR capability | Playbooks created | 0 to 12 |
| Formalize policy library | Approved policies | 0 to 14 |

### 4. Governance Structure — Approved

- **Executive Sponsor:** CISO (Sarah Chen)
- **Technical Sponsor:** VP IT (Marcus Johnson)
- **Risk Sponsor:** CRO (Emily Rodriguez)
- **Project Lead:** Venkata Bharath Kasyap Devulapalli
- **Steering Committee:** CISO, CRO, VP IT, Finance — bi-weekly cadence
- **Working Group:** Project Lead + team leads — weekly cadence

### 5. Timeline — Approved with Modification

52-week timeline approved with one change:
- Phase 4 (Policies & Controls) extended by 2 weeks for Legal review (total: 10 weeks)
- No change to overall 52-week timeline; absorbed through parallel workstreams

### 6. Budget — Approved

- $2.4M total budget approved
- Q1 budget release: $600K
- Monthly reforecasting required
- Capital/operating split: 60/40
