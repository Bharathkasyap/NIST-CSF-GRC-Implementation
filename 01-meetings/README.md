## Meeting Cadence

| Meeting Type | Frequency | Attendees | Purpose |
|-------------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Project Status | Weekly | Project team | Track progress, resolve blockers |
| Steering Committee | Bi-weekly | Sponsors, leads | Decision-making, escalation |
| Phase Gate Review | Per phase | All stakeholders | Approve phase completion |
| Board Presentation | Quarterly | Board, C-suite | Executive visibility |
| Risk Committee | Monthly | CISO, Risk team | Risk posture updates |

## Phase Gate Criteria

Each phase gate review validates:
1. All deliverables completed and reviewed
2. Quality criteria met
3. Risks and issues documented and mitigated
4. Budget and schedule on track
5. Stakeholder sign-off obtained
6. Lessons learned captured
